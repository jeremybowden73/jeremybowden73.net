Anagram demo

The top half of this embedded window is the code, so probably best not to touch that unless you know what you're doing. If it goes pear-shaped just reload the page (Ctrl-R or Cmd-R).


In the lower half, click the "Play" triangle (bottom right corner).


After the instructions pop up, enter up to 12 letters and hit return (you can enter spaces, numbers, or characters but these will be removed by the program).
Depending on your browser you might need to click in the lower half of the window to activate the cursor.
You will be rewarded with a list of "phrases" (strings of words) that are exact anagrams of the letters you entered.
