About Jeremy
The Past

In the 1990s I graduated with an Honours degree in Engineering. This led to gainful employment in the engineering sector, working with companies such as Ford, Caterpillar, and Dunlop.

I acquired some skills that helped me do my job...

  • Expert 3D CAD/CAE skills
  • Expert in PLM (Product Lifecycle Management; essentially GitHub for engineers and product designers)
  • Great knowledge of Excel
  • Great engineering knowledge, like knowing the endurance stress limit for most steels is roughly 50% of the tensile strength

And some that helped make me more valuable to the my colleagues...

  • Presenting and describing my ideas
  • Communicating with peers both informally and in formal reviews
  • Making and implementing contingency plans, when things went a little bit wonky
  • Adapting to established company cultures
  • Helping others to develop skills when I had greater knowledge or experience
  • Developing a good sense of when to relinquish and when to hold the line
