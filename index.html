A bit more about me
Read on to find out more about me.
But before all that, click here to view my CV.

I hold a degree in Engineering Design, and have 20 years' professional experience in Mechanical Engineering, working with companies like Caterpillar, Ford, Dunlop, and GE.


After enforced redundancy in 2017, an interest in computing and programming led me to seek a fresh start in the technology sector, which I find exciting and rewarding. I'm self-teaching, using resources that develop all-round coding skills as well as language syntax.

I hope this will enable me to find a role in development in either front-end, back-end, full-stack, or in data analysis, where I can apply my zest for problem-solving through code.

I’m able to bring a range of transferable technical skills, for example analytical skills and attention to detail, as well as numerous "soft skills": maintaining business documents, communicating within global teams, and being a productive contributor to team discussions to name just some.
